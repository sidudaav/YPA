Chapters

Chapters allow for students and schools to be a part of YPA. Schools can set up chapters in order to officially be recognized by YPA. By creating chapters, schools have an organized medium to spread and enjoy physics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Registering a Chapter Cost Money?
No, registering a chapter is completely free!
When Should I Start a Chapter?
A chapter is a great thing to start if you feel that your school lacks opportunity for students interested in Physics. If you want to take the initiative to solve this issue, starting a YPA chapter is a great solution!
How Do I Maintain a Chapter?
Staring a chapter is a lot of fun, but it is also very challenging. You should start a chapter in your school only if you feel that you are willing to take on a serious leadership role and if you have a team of dedicated students backing your initiative. To maintain a chapter, remember to hold weekly or bi-weekly meetings and, most importantly, keep physics fun! If you experience any difficulties, do not be afraid to contact us.
