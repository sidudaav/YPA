Welcome to YPA!

The Young Physcist's Association, or YPA for short, is a student run organization dedicated to the spread of physics across High Schools around the world. We host various events and allow for students to start YPA Chapters in their own schools.

Why Should I Join YPA?

That's a great question! YPA is an organization with the goal of spreading physics to high schools all over the world. By creating or beign a member of a chapter, competing, or even just telling others about us, you help this mission succeed. The lack of a centralized physics organization for high schoolers is troubling. Students who are interested in physics deserve the opportunity to be able to study or participate in physics related activities at a deeper level. That is what YPA is all about. We want to reach as many students as possible and allow them to do whatever they are passionate about, whether it is participating in quiz bowls with their friend, taking written competitions, or even just talking with others about the subject which brings us all together.
