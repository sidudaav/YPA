Physics Speed Bowl

Welcome to YPA's first ever event! Physics Speed Bowl(PSB) is a Quiz Bowl style contest bringing together interested and talented physics students. The competition will take place through Zoom on July 10th 2020. The winning team will receive $150.

Prizes

  1. The winning team will recieve 150 USD.

  4. Only one member per team should fill out the registration form.
  5. After registering, you will receive a message confirming your registration within a couple days.

Rules

  1. Each team will consist of 1-4 high school students.
    • Students do not need to be in the same school to be on a team.
  2. Each round will consist of 15 pairs of toss up and bonus questions.
    • Each toss up question is worth 4 points and each bonus question is worth 10 points.
    • Patricipants may answer the question before it is fully read, but an incorrect answer will give the opposing team 4 points and the chance to answer.
    • Toss up questions are given 20 seconds and bonus questions are given 30 seconds.
    • Toss up questions must be answered individually, but teams may confer for bonus questions.
  3. After all questions have been asked, the winner will be determined by the team with the most points.
  4. If the score is tied after all questions, the winner will be determined by the team which answered the last question correctly.
  5. The tournament bracket will be randomly generated.
    • The format of the tournament is single elimination.
