Physics Speed Bowl

Welcome to YPA's first ever event! Physics Speed Bowl(PSB) is a Quiz Bowl style contest brining together interested and talented physics students. The competition will take place through Zoom on July 10th 2020. The winning team will receive $150.

Register

  1. Click this link to register
  2. Registration must be completed by the end of July 5th 2020.
  3. Only one member per team should fill out the registration form.
  4. After registering, you will receive a message confirming your registration within a couple days.

Rules

  1. Each team will consist of 1-4 high school students.
    • Students do not need to be in the same school to be on a team.
  2. Each round will consist of 20 questions.
    • Each question is one point.
    • After 20 questions have been asked, the winner will be determined by the team with the most points.
    • If the score is tied after 20 questions, the winner will be determined by the team which answered the last question correctly.
  3. Questions will be at the difficulty of an advanced high school physics class.
    • All questions will be free response and multiple choice.
    • Each question will be given 30 seconds to be answered.
    • Students will have to answer each question without discussing with their team members unless it is a bonus question.
    • If one team answers a question incorrectly, the opposing team will get a chance to answer.
    • Students may interrupt while a question is being read. However, they will not get to hear the full question. If the interrupted answer is incorrect, one point will be lost.
