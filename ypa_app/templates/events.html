Events

YPA hosts a number of events throughout both the school year and the summer. These events allow students to network and come together to have a great experience solving physics problems or participating in challenges.

Think Physics! 2020

End Date: September 18th

Considering you are reading this, a lot of your experience in physics is likely centered around solving problems from textbooks or tests and contests. Now it is your chance to go a step forward and solve a real world problem of your choice. In YPA’s next contest, write a research paper that proposes a hypothetical solution backed by physics that aims to make progress in solving some type of issue, whether it is a global issue or a small one in our daily lives.

Code-ify Physics! 2020

End Date: September 18th

Love coding and love physics? For YPA’s third ever contest we are hosting the Code-ify Physics contest which will allow you to do both at the same time! You will get the opportunity to code any physics related ideas you want for a chance to win prizes. From an educational program to a physics simulation, the sky’s the limit!

Azerbaijan Physics Bowl

Date: August 30th

YPA has partnered with the Ministry of Education in Azerbaijan to host the National Physics Bowl competition of Azerbaijan (PhBA). PhBA is a quiz bowl of similar format to that of Physics Speed Bowl. In order to register a team for this event, you must be a citizen of Azerbaijan. More details for the competition can be found on the YPA Azerbaijan website.

Physics Speed Bowl (Completed)

Date: July 10th, 3:00 PM EDT

The Physics Speed Bowl (PSB) was the first ever event hosted by YPA, and it was a huge success! After receiving high interest for the event, we selected the first 32 teams to register for the event. In PSB, teams from across the world faced off against each other to fight out the problems in a high paced quiz bowl type format. Congratulations to our winners Blairlake(USA), finalist Chandrashekar Limit (USA), semi-finalist Allen Ahemdabad (IND), and semi-finalist Annihilators (AZE), for being the top 4 teams in the competition!
