Welcome to YPA!

The Young Physicists Association (YPA) is a student-run organization that provides opportunities and events for getting more high schoolers across the world interested in physics.

Why Should I Join YPA?

That's a great question! YPA is an organization with the goal of spreading an interest in physics to high schools all over the world. By creating or being a member of a chapter, competing, or even just telling others about us, you help this mission succeed. YPA serves as a centralized physics organization for high school students, something not present in the past. We want to reach as many students as possible and allow them to participate in activities they enjoy, whether it is participating in quiz bowls with their friend, taking written competitions, or even just talking with others about the subject which brings us all together.

What Events Do We Host?

YPA aims to host a large variety of events ranging from test events to paper sumbissions. Regardless of what contest is occuring, YPA will stay true to its goal of making physics contests more accessible for students who have a deep interest in physics.
